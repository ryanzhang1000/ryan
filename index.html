The Perfect PB & J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Any peanut butter and jam will work but my recommendation is to use grape jelly and all natural peanut butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jay of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of all natural peanut butter

Directions

1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.

2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut buter on the left side.

3. On the right side, spread 1/8 inch layer of jelly.

4. Carefully place the two halves together sp that the jelly is on top.
